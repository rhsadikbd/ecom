Our Story

Quality Livestock Limited (QLL) was founded in 2021, with the objective of sustainable farming, processing and marketing premium grade cattle, mutton and chicken products. Production of high-quality livestock has always been at the core of our business principles and QLL serves as an extension in our mission to deliver safe and hygienic protein to our customers.

QLL entails a farming setup with a capacity of 2500 cattle for beef fattening. The production unit consists of a state-of-the-art processing plant to ensure safe, halal and hygienic meat.

Sustainability and zero waste in all our business ventures are an integral part of our core vision, and QLL is no exception in this regard with our policy of being carbon neutral and independent of external power source. The project will be run on solar power and fitted with an ETP (water treatment) and a Biogas setup.

The project is situated in Nandigram, Bogura on 126 bighas of land.
